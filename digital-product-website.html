What is a Digital Product Website?

A digital product website is an online store designed to sell non-physical products like:

  • eBooks
  • Online courses
  • Templates, PDFs, Notion docs, or digital planners
  • Video tutorials or mini workshops

With WordPress, you can create a professional-looking digital product store on a small budget — ideal for beginners, creators, and small businesses.
